Overview of the Contract farmer-led Spokes

At the heart of our mission to empower communities is the Contract farmer-led Spokes Initiative—a groundbreaking program designed to uplift and support women in agriculture.

These "mamapreneurs" are entrepreneurial women who serve as key agents of change in their communities. By providing them with access to resources, training, and mentorship, we enable them to lead localized hubs ("spokes") that drive economic growth, improve nutrition, and foster sustainable farming practices.
